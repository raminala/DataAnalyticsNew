  1. Introduction:

In this post, the solution for assignment 01 is provided. I selected “ChickWeight” dataset because it consists of both Numerical and Categorical data. This data frame has 578 rows and 4 columns from an experiment on the effect of diet on early growth of chicks. This dataset has two numeric variables:

weight: weight of specific chick at specific day (Time), and

Time: Number of days since birth to measurement day,

as well as two ordered variables:

Chick: identifier of chick. from 1 to 48, and

Diet: 4 different applied diets.
