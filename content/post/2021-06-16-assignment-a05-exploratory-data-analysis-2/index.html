Import Data: Energy & Mining

This post consists of exploratory analysis of a dataset provided by the world bank. It includes 53 variable and 513 observations that represent many countries around the world between 1960 to 2017. Variables include varieties of parameters related to welfare and sustainability in examined countries. These variables include but not limited to access and consumption of different types of energies from different sources.
